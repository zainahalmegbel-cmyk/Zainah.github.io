Profile

A voice shaped
by two worlds

I'm a journalist and creative with a background spanning print, broadcast, digital media, and brand communications. My work is grounded in critical media theory and driven by a commitment to storytelling that centres underrepresented perspectives.

Trained at Concordia University in Journalism and currently studying Media & Culture at the University of Amsterdam, I've built my practice across newsrooms, event spaces, studio sets, and community organisations.

As a founding board member of ASAA, I've seen what it means to build something from nothing — a brand, a community, a platform — and I bring that founding energy to every project I take on.
